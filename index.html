Akan name Finder

Welcome to my web application that requests for your birthday details and gender then calculates the day you were born. Depending on your gender Your Akan name will be displayed. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana,
children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
